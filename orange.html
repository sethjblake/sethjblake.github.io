Dragon Breeds



ORANGE

Big dragon hit hard.

Difficulty: SIMPLE
Archetype: BEAT-DOWN

QUESTS:

[Left] Have a dragon with 4 or more SV in the battlefield.
[Middle] Gain a Faction Coin
[Right] Trash a card from your hand

Note: The Orange player can complete their leftmost quest multiple times in one turn by having multiple 4+ SV dragons in the battlefield.

OBSIDIAN

AMETHYST

Amethyst's ability only triggers when it is attacking.

If a player's Player Deck is a total of exactly 10 cards, none of their cards can be trashed.

DIAMOND DRAGON

Diamond Dragon's ability only triggers when it is attacking.

CRUSHER

Having multiple Crushers in the battlefield stacks their abilities on top of each other. However, Crusher does not buff itself with its own ability. Other Crushers can, though, buff other Crushers.

BERZERKER

Every time a blocking dragon or an additional blocking dragon is assigned to Berzerker, its ability triggers.

ROCKSLIDE

Defending players can block Rockslide with their dragons, but Rockslide cannot deal its damage to any of them. Rockslide must deal its combat damage to the defending player when it is attacking.

QUAKE

MAGMA DRAGON

Magma Dragon's ability only triggers when it gains SV by a dragon's ability. If it gains SV by another source, it does not give its owner a Fireball Token.
