ORANGE

Big dragon hit hard.

Difficulty: SIMPLE
Archetype: BEAT-DOWN


STRATEGY GUIDE

Orange is easily the most beginner-friendly faction, while its straightforward mechanics allow for some more difficult combos for more advanced players picking up the old 'dumb and ugly' faction. While there are some Orange dragons that don't combo, any player is safe buying a mix of dragons. Also, though Orange can be reliant on its direct-damage dealing Rockslide dragon to take out enemies in later rounds of the game, players have the choice of putting all their eggs in their Legendary dragon's basket by choosing Obsidian, which is a great early-game unlock and finisher.

Note that, though Orange dragons pack a big punch, Orange can have some trouble ramping its dragon count as quickly as other factions, so as players learn to balance their coin aquisition and trashing actions they can become more and more of a single-minded killing machine.
