Dragon Breeds



GREEN

Most Green dragons are gentle in the wild, at least, until their hives are disturbed.

Difficulty: INTERMEDIATE
Archetype: HYPER-CARRY

QUESTS:

[Left] Buy a dragon.
[Middle] Gain a Faction Coin
[Right] Trash a card from your hand

Note: If a player purchases multiple dragons in the same turn, they move their leftmost quest up that many times.

SWARM DRAGON

Token Dragons that survive defense remain in front of their owner. Token Dragons that die, or are still posessed at the end of a player's own draw phase, return to their source.

While a dragon is taken from a Supply as a token card, its spot in the Supply is not restocked. When that dragon is lost/dies, it returns to that spot in the Supply.

NOTE: Token cards cannot be trashed or discarded by player actions or dragon abilities.

FOSSILIZED

If a dragon doubles Fossilized ability, its owner can trash two cards.

GUARDIAN

Note: When a player plays a Fireball Token, return it to its source after combat.

STAMPEDE

Stampede's ability only triggers when it is attacking.

It is recommended to attack with a Stampede with (if possible) other Stampede's available in the Green Player's Supply to gain as token cards.

It is also recommended to attack with Stampede and at least one Queen Dragon, if able.

QUEEN

VENOMOUS

Venomous does not increase dragons' SV, so their health does not increase. It only increases the damage those dragons do to other dragons.

If multiple Venomous dragons are in the battlefield, their damage-increase abilities add together.

SCAVENGER

When scavenger's ability is doubled, its owner must discard multiple cards to get multiple sets of coins from it.

NOTE: This is a newer ruling, based on the wording of the card, to make the game more consistent.
