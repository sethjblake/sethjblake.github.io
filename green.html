Dragon Breeds

GREEN

Most Green dragons are gentle in the wild, at least, until their hives are disturbed.

Difficulty: INTERMEDIATE
Archetype: HYPER-CARRY


STRATEGY GUIDE

Green has the most unique ability synergy in the game of Dragon Breeds. While it does have the capabilities of swarming around enemy defenses, it's not a pure swarm deck. In fact, there is only one dragon that can allow it to pop off into a near-garunteed to be lethal attack: its Epic dragon, Stampede, which also happens to be the most expensive card to purchase in the game.

The archetype "HYPER-CARRY" comes from Green's reliance on Stampede in order to take other players out. Until Green buys Stampede, it is easily the most passive faction in the game. Most of its dragons don't cause anything to happen directly in the battlefield, but have abilities that affect player's purchasing abilities by making things easier for Green and harder for opponents.

However, once Green gets is Stampede, it usually doesn't need to buy any more dragons. The more efficient the deck can become, and the more often the Green player is able to draw their win condition, the better chance they have at hitting their opponents hard and taking home the crown.
