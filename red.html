Dragon Breeds



RED

Bulldoze through defenders and reduce your opponents to ash.

Difficulty: MODERATE
Archetype: BEAT-DOWN

QUESTS:

[Left] Kill a dragon with fireballs
[Middle] Gain a Faction Coin
[Right] Trash a card from your hand

Note: To kill a dragon with fireballs, the red player must reduice its SV to zero with the use of a Fireball. Death by combat damage does not count. Also, if the Red player kills multiple enemy dragons with fireballs in the same turn, they complete their quest multiple times and move their tracker up their quest card accordingly.

BURN

Burn is the only dragon in the game with the capability to kill on defense.

HYDRA

Hydra's ability only takes effect when it is attacking.

SMOULDER

VOLCANIC

Volcanic only gives its owner's fireballs +1 damage if it is attacking.

Playing multiple Volcanics in one turn does not cause Fireballs to do more than +1 damage.

ECLIPSE

Eclipse's ability only triggers when it is attacking.

GEYSER

Attacking with multiple Geysers does not cause attacking dragons to deal more than +1 damage to players.

Geyser causes attacking dragons to deal +1 damage to players, not dragons.

BURST

Token cards gained on defense last until their owner's next turn. Players lose their tokens at their draw phase on their own turn when they discard their hand and re-draw.

FLASH DRAGON

FUELER

Epic Coin Tokens can count toward the purchase of a dragon of any rarity. However, at least one Faction Coin is required per dragon purchase.

If multiple Fuelers are in the battlefield when one enemy dragon dies by fireballs, each of them triggers their secondary ability once.
