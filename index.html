Welcome to the Dragon Breeds Universe

Hi. My name is Seth Blake. I am a student at the University of Michigan studying computer science' and Dragon Breeds has been my main side project since I was looking for ways to keep myself busy and entertain my younger siblings during the spring and summer of 2020. Since then, a handful of friends, family, and I have iterated the game design and spent an uncountable number of hours bringing the game to life with our artwork and graphic design. So far, we've released our first set of the many we've designed, Dragon Breeds: OVERWORLD, and the game has brought in over $1000 in gross revenue and has been purchased by early playtesters in multiple states accross the US.
