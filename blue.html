Dragon Breeds



BLUE

Playing Blue can feel like a long-shot at times... but when it pays off, it goes big.

Difficulty: INTERMEDIATE
Archetype: SWARM

QUESTS:

[Left] Draw two cards in the same turn by dragons' abilities.
[Middle] Gain a Faction Coin
[Right] Trash a card from your hand

Note: If the Blue player draws 4 or 6 cards in a single turn by dragons' abilities, they move their leftmost quest up multiple times.

WRECKAGE

If a player's Player Deck is a total of exactly 10 cards, none of their cards can be trashed.
